Among Us Web

Play the classic social deduction game right in your browser.

PLAY

How to Play

To start the game, the host has to click the "start" button. At the beginning of a game, each player is either assigned the "Crewmate" or the "Impostor" role. In rooms with more than 6 players, there will be 2 impostors - in smaller rooms will be 1 impostor. Crewmates have to complete tasks, report dead bodies and eject the impostor. They can also check the admin table to look for suspicious activity. Impostors are supposed to blend in with the crew, pretend to run tasks, and kill off the crew. They are also able to sneak through vents. The game ends as soon as all impostors are dead (victory for crew), or there are as many living impostors as crewmates (victory for impostors). (chat is only for computer.)

Controls

  • [WASD] / [Arrow Keys] — Move
  • [Space] — Use / Vent (Impostor)
  • [R] — Report Body
  • [Q] — Kill (Impostor)
